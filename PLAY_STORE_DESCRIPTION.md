## FAQ

**Q: Is my location data safe?**
A: Yes. All location data is encrypted in transit and at rest. It's never shared with third parties without consent. You can disable tracking anytime.

**Q: What payment methods are accepted?**
A: We accept credit cards, debit cards, UPI, and mobile wallets (Google Pay, PhonePe, Paytm).

**Q: Can I use this without a smartphone?**
A: You can also access your account via web browser at https://school-bus-fee-management-system.vercel.app

**Q: Is there a fee for parents to use this app?**
A: No. The app is free for parents. Schools/transport companies may have fees configured by their administrators.

**Q: How often is the location updated?**
A: Location updates every 30 seconds when tracking is enabled.

**Q: Can I give access to multiple family members?**
A: Yes. You can add links for other parents/guardians from the app settings.

**Q: What happens to my data if I delete the app?**
A: Your data remains in our servers. You can request permanent deletion anytime via the app's privacy settings.

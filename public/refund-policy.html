Refund & Cancellation Policy

BusWay Pro — School Bus Fee Management System
Website: https://school-bus-fee-management-system.vercel.app
Last Updated: April 2026

1. Overview

BusWay Pro is a digital platform that enables parents to pay school bus transportation fees online. All payments made through this platform are for school bus transportation services provided by the respective school/institution. This policy outlines the conditions under which refunds and cancellations are processed.

2. Refund Eligibility

Refunds may be issued under the following circumstances:

  • Duplicate Payment: If a parent is charged more than once for the same billing period due to a technical error, the duplicate amount will be refunded in full.
  • Excess Payment: If the amount charged exceeds the actual fee for the billing period, the excess amount will be refunded.
  • Service Not Provided: If the school bus transportation service is permanently discontinued for a student (e.g., student withdrawal, route cancellation), a pro-rata refund for the unused period may be issued subject to school administration approval.
  • Failed Transaction with Amount Debited: If a payment fails but the amount is debited from the parent's account, the amount will be automatically refunded by the payment gateway (PayU) within 5-7 business days.

3. Non-Refundable Scenarios

  • The student has availed the bus transportation service for the billed month/period.
  • Voluntary absence from the bus service by the student (the seat remains reserved).
  • Late fee or penalty charges that were correctly applied as per the school's fee policy.
  • Change of mind after a successful payment for a valid billing cycle.

4. Refund Process & Timeline

Step 1 - Request: Parent submits a refund request via email to support@buswaypro.app with the transaction ID, student name, and reason.

Step 2 - Review: The school administration reviews the request within 3-5 business days.

Step 3 - Processing: If approved, the refund is initiated via the original payment method (PayU gateway).

Step 4 - Credit: Refund is credited to the parent's original payment source within 7-10 business days from approval.

5. Cancellation Policy

  • Before Payment: Parents may cancel at any time before completing the payment. No charges are applied.
  • After Payment: Once a payment is successfully processed for a billing cycle, it cannot be cancelled. However, the parent may request a refund if eligible as per Section 2.
  • Service Cancellation by School: If the school discontinues bus service, affected parents will receive a refund for the unused portion.

6. How to Request a Refund

Email: support@buswaypro.app

Please include your Transaction ID, Student Name, Admission Number, and Reason for Refund.

7. Payment Gateway

All online payments are securely processed through PayU, a PCI DSS compliant payment gateway. PayU supports Credit Cards, Debit Cards, UPI (Google Pay, PhonePe, BHIM), Net Banking, and Wallets. BusWay Pro does not store any card or bank account details.

1. Nature of Service

BusWay Pro is a digital platform that provides an online fee payment and school bus management service. This is not a physical product — no physical goods are shipped or delivered.

What BusWay Pro provides:

  • Online payment of monthly school bus transportation fees
  • Digital payment receipts (downloadable PDF)
  • Real-time bus tracking for parents
  • Fee history and billing records
  • Notifications and alerts

2. Service Delivery

Since BusWay Pro is a digital service, all deliverables are provided electronically and instantly:

  • Payment Confirmation: Delivered instantly upon successful payment via on-screen confirmation and in-app notification.
  • Digital Receipt: Available for download immediately after payment. Also accessible anytime via the Receipts section of the app.
  • Fee Status Update: The student's fee record is updated in real-time to reflect "PAID" status in both parent and admin dashboards.
  • Email Notification: Payment confirmation email sent to the parent's registered email address within minutes of successful payment (if enabled).

3. Bus Transportation Service

The actual school bus transportation service is provided by the school/institution, not by BusWay Pro. BusWay Pro acts solely as a technology platform to facilitate fee collection, tracking, and communication between schools and parents.

  • Bus routes, schedules, and operations are managed by the school administration.
  • Any queries regarding bus service should be directed to the school's bus administration office.
  • BusWay Pro provides real-time GPS tracking of buses as an informational service.

4. Service Availability

  • Platform: BusWay Pro is accessible 24/7 via web browser and Android mobile app.
  • Coverage: Available across India wherever the partnered school operates bus services.
  • Downtime: Scheduled maintenance, if any, will be communicated via in-app notifications. We aim for 99.9% uptime.

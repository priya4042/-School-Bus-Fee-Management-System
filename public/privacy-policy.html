Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 22, 2026

1. Introduction

BusWay Pro ("we," "us," "our," or "Company") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application (the "App").

2. Information We Collect

We may collect information about you in a variety of ways. The information we may collect on the App includes:

  • Camera Permission: Used for livestreaming bus camera feeds for monitoring purposes
  • Location Data: GPS data used to track real-time bus location and route information
  • Student Information: Name, ID, class, associated parent/guardian details, linked to your account
  • Financial Information: Payment details for school bus fees (processed via Razorpay and Stripe)
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system version, app version
  • Usage Information: How you interact with the app, features used, notifications received

3. Use of Your Information

Having accurate information about you permits us to provide you with a smooth, efficient, and customized experience. Specifically, we may use information collected about you via the App to:

  • Display live bus location tracking for your assigned bus routes
  • Stream bus camera feeds for safety and monitoring purposes
  • Calculate and display monthly bus fee dues
  • Process payments through our integrated payment gateways
  • Send notifications about schedules, delays, and fee reminders
  • Generate fee reports, receipts, and transaction history
  • Manage student attendance records for pickup and drop-off
  • Maintain audit logs for administrative purposes
  • Improve app functionality and user experience
  • Ensure app security and prevent fraud

4. Disclosure of Your Information

We do not sell, trade, or rent your information to third parties. However, we may disclose your information when:

  • Payment Processing: Information is shared with Razorpay and Stripe for payment processing
  • Communication: Information is shared with Twilio for SMS/OTP services
  • Maps Services: Location data is shared with Google Maps for location services
  • Law Enforcement: Disclosure is required by law or to protect rights and safety
  • School Administrators: Relevant information shared with authorized school personnel

5. Security of Your Information

We use administrative, technical, and physical security measures to protect your personal information. However, no method of transmission over the Internet or method of electronic storage is 100% secure. We strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your personal information, but we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

## Submission Instructions

When submitting to IARC/Google Play:

1. **Access Content Rating Questionnaire:**
   - Go to Google Play Console
   - Select "Content rating" in left menu
   - Click "Fill out questionnaire"

2. **Fill Questionnaire:**
   - Copy responses from this document
   - Select corresponding answers in the form
   - Provide explanations where prompted

3. **Submit for Rating:**
   - Click "Save & Validate"
   - Google will assign ratings automatically
   - Usually takes 1-2 hours

4. **Verify Results:**
   - Confirm ratings across ESRB, PEGI, etc.
   - All should show age-appropriate ratings above

5. **Publish App:**
   - Proceed with app listing publication
   - Ratings will display on Play Store
